SRIDs

A standard set of 1000's of spatial reference identifiers set by the Open Geospatial Consortium (OGC)

Used to share geospatial data between apps

Universal Transverse Mercator (UTM)

SRID's covering a range of spatial reference around the globe. Locally global.

Web Geodetic System (WGS84 / SRID 4326)

This is what Google Maps and your GPS uses. Less accurate but global.
